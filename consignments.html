Security Firearm Auctions (“Auctioneer”) conducts public, online auctions for firearms, ammunition, and related items. By signing this agreement, the Seller engages Auctioneer to consign and sell the items listed, subject to the terms below.

Download PDF Open PDF

Fees & Commission

  • $2.00 per lot (listing fee).
  • Commission schedule (per item, by hammer price):
    • $10 and under: 30%
    • $11 – $499: 10%
    • $500 – $999: 5%
    • $1,000 and above: 0%
  • Buy-back / No-Sale fee: If Seller buys back their own item, a fee equal to the buyer’s premium (10%) applies.
  • Reserve items: $20 per reserved lot due at drop-off (credited back if the item sells).

Sale Terms

  • Seller appoints Security Firearm Auctions as their agent to conduct a public auction and authorizes actions reasonably necessary to complete the sale.
  • Items are sold to the highest bidder. Auctioneer may group or split lots at its discretion if not otherwise specified by Seller.
  • All items remain in Auctioneer’s possession until the buyer’s payment clears. Seller is not paid for any item for which Auctioneer is not paid.
  • If Seller’s property is misrepresented, Auctioneer may withhold or adjust payment to Seller.

Owner’s Warranties

  • Seller warrants they are the lawful owner with merchantable title and authorizes Auctioneer to convey good title to the buyer.
  • Seller acknowledges responsibility for the items and agrees to maintain adequate insurance coverage for their property; Auctioneer bears no responsibility for loss or damage to Seller’s personal property.

Compliance & Transfers

  • All firearm sales/ transfers must comply with federal, state, and local law, including background checks and FFL handling as applicable.
